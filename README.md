# Beonmap 🧭

**Where Potential Becomes Opportunity**

Beonmap is a map-based people discovery platform that helps the right people find each other.  
It reveals potential by making professionals, entrepreneurs, and opportunities visible on an interactive map.

🌐 https://beonmap.com

---

## Overview

Opportunities often exist nearby — but the right people rarely discover each other.

Beonmap solves this by creating a **real-time professional discovery map** where users can explore people, ideas, and opportunities around them.

Instead of searching through endless platforms, users can simply **open a map and discover who is around them**.

---

## Core Idea

> Opportunity begins with presence.

Beonmap reveals potential by making the right people visible to each other.

Entrepreneurs find collaborators.  
Professionals get discovered.  
Businesses connect with talent and clients.

All through a **simple map interface**.

---

## Features

### 🗺 Interactive Geo-Location Map
Discover professionals, businesses, and opportunities around you in real-time.

### 🤝 Real-Time Networking
Instantly connect with entrepreneurs, collaborators, investors, and professionals.

### 👤 Auto Generated Professional Portfolio
Users automatically get a professional profile that showcases their skills, experience, and expertise.

### 🏢 White Label Solutions
Organizations can deploy their own branded discovery maps to connect talent, clients, and teams.
